The City of Aberdeen

'Bon Accord'

Quick Facts:

  • 3rd largest city in Scotland
  • Oil Capital of Europe
  • Population of around 200,000
  • A university over 600 years old

Information

Aberdeen is a hub for European oil and gas due to it's proximity to the North Sea and excellent transport links. This has brought a lot of prosperity to the area, with Aberdeen making up 17% of Scotland's GDP and having the top two wealthiest areas in Scotland. It also happens to be the home of Aberdeen FC, the most successful Scottish football team in European competitions.

Located in the North East of Scotland
